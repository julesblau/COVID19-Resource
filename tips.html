Steps You Can Take to Slow the Spread of COVID-19
Wear a Mask You can be an asymptomatic carrier of the disease. Wearing a mask protects others!
Keep a Safe Distance from Others Large groups of people are the easiest way to spread the disease. Avoiding large gatherings greatly reduces the spread of COVID-19.
Cover your Cough or Sneeze with a Tissue The virus is airborn, it is critical to cover your mouth with a tissue when coughing or sneezing. Immediately throw away tissue afterwards.
Avoid Touching Your Face Practicing good hygeine and not touching your face is an easy way to prevent contracting the disease
Clean your Hands Often Washing your hands with soap and water for 20 seconds greatly reduces the spread of the virus
